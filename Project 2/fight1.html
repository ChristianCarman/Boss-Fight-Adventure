BOSS FIGHT

As you begin to make the final distance towards the boss you begin to ready your sword with a plan to make the first hit. You go in with little knowledge of what to expect from this boss as little to no information has been gathered about them.

As you finally get close enough to the boss you can finally see that it looks like a pile of rocks waiting for your sword to slice through it and allow your country to regain control over this fallen region

The once pile of rocks begins to build up into what looks like a human body, a Golem, it has yet to notice you as you are but small and insufficient for it to care. The Golem stands at a height of what you can only imagine to be 50 feet tall, well you are a mere 5'8. You plan to try and hit the Golem in a weak spot but you aren't sure where to hit first.
